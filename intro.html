This application visualises the English Indices of Deprivation 2019 for Lower-layer Super Output Areas (LSOA) within Trafford. Each LSOA contains approximately 1,500 residents or 650 households. The are ranked from 1 (most deprived) to 32,844 (least deprived) and assigned to a decile ranging from 1 (most deprived 10%) to 10 (least deprived 10%). This infographic published by the MHCLG provides more information.
